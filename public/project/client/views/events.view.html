Events at Knight Moves Somerville

Meetup Monday!

Every other Monday is a night for open table gaming (with optional nametags) where you are encouraged to come on your own and play games with other members of the community. No experience needed, we’re happy to teach you!

Ladies Knight!

Ladies Knight is a night of gaming that focuses on and celebrates the awesome women in our gaming community. If you’re into board games (or would like to be) and you identify as a woman in a way that’s significant to you, come on down and hang out with us! We’d love to meet you. Dates are available at the links below.


We also host additional nights of open-table gaming and one-time events and parties.
