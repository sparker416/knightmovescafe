Q: Do you take reservations?
A: No, but you can rent a room for an event. Contact us to discuss options.

Q: When do I pay?
A: You can pay as you enter or as you leave.

Q: How do I pay?
A: We accept all forms of payment including cash, check, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

Q: Can I bring my own food?
A: Absolutely! There is an extra $2.00 charge for any outside food or drink. This fee is included if you have a monthly membership.

Q: Can I bring my own games?
A: Yes, we encourage it.

Q: Do I need to know how to play any games?
A: No. If you already know how to play, great! If you don't, we'll teach you!

Q: Do you sell games?
A: No, but check out Eureka! down the street from our Brookline location.

Q: Are your games for kids or adults?
A: Knight Moves is for everyone!
