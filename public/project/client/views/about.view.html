Who We Are

Knight Moves currently offers New England’s only board game cafés! We offer a relaxed atmosphere to work, or play games with family and friends. We have a massive board game library accented with our knowledgeable staff to help you learn any game. Don’t forget to ask about our organic, fair trade drinks and snacks!

Game Library

Knight Moves has, collectively, over 1,000 games to choose from, and we’re adding new ones all the time! So whether you’re looking for an old-school classic like Risk, a Euro game favorite like Settlers of Catan, or an off-the-wall party game like Cards Against Humanity, we’ve got something you’ll love!

Events

Knight Moves hosts events for gamers and non-gamers alike, from birthday parties to open mic nights. The cafe has plenty of room to accommodate both small and large parties and rooms can be rented for private events. A party at Knight Moves is a unique and fun experience for all ages!
