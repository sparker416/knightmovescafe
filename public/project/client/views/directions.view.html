Knight Moves Brookline

Knight Moves Cafe
1402 Beacon Street
Brookline, MA 02446
(617) 487-5259

By MBTA:
Take the Green Line "C" Branch to the Winchester Street/Summit Avenue stop.

Parking:
There is on-street parking along Beacon Street for $1 per hour, and limited free parking around the corner on Summit Ave. There is also a metered public parking lot located on Centre Street. Meters are free after 8:00PM and on Sundays and holidays.

Knight Moves Somerville

Knight Moves Cafe
1159 Broadway
Somerville, MA 02144
(617) 702-4726

By MBTA:
Take the Red Line to the Davis Square stop. From there, you can walk up Holland Street to where it intersects with Broadway. Knight Moves will be on your right. You can also take the 87 or 88 bus towards Clarendon Hill to the Broadway @ Curtis Street - Teele Square stop.

Parking:
There is a metered public parking lot on Herbert Street where it intersects with Day Street, another at the intersection of Grove Street and Highland Avenue, another on Buena Vista Road off of Holland Street, a fourth off of Grove Street in between Elm Street and Highland Avenue, and a fifth called the Cutter Street Lot. Meters are free after 8:00PM and on Sundays and holidays. Please see this map from www.parksomerville.com.
