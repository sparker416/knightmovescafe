About

Knight Moves is a board game café, with one location in Brookline, MA and another in Somerville, MA. The cafés offer coffee, snacks, and massive board game libraries. Customers can enjoy a comfortable space, with knowledgeable staff and a relaxed atmosphere. An evening at Knight Moves is hassle-free entertainment and a chance to spend time face-to-face with family and friends.
